# Telco Customer Retention Master Playbook

This document outlines professional strategies for reducing customer churn in the telecom industry. The AI Retention Agent uses this knowledge base to generate personalized intervention plans.

## 1. Contract & Billing Strategies

### High Risk: Month-to-Month Contracts
*   **Strategy**: Transition to Long-term commitment.
*   **Tactics**:
    *   Offer a 15% discount for a 1-year contract.
    *   Waive activation fees for 2-year commitments.
    *   Highlight the "Price Lock Guarantee" for multi-year plans.
*   **Rationale**: Customers on month-to-month plans have the highest churn rate due to low switching costs.

### Billing Issues & Paperless Billing
*   **Strategy**: Incentivize stable payment methods.
*   **Tactics**:
    *   Offer a $5/month credit for enabling Auto-pay and Paperless Billing.
    *   Provide a "Billing Health Check" call for customers with frequent manual payments.
*   **Rationale**: Automatic payments reduce "transactional friction" and late fee disputes.

## 2. Service & Technical Support

### Lack of Online Security/Backup
*   **Strategy**: Value-added bundling.
*   **Tactics**:
    *   Provide 3 months of free Online Security or Cloud Backup.
    *   Include "Security Essentials" bundle for Fiber Optic users at a discounted rate.
*   **Rationale**: Customers using more integrated services (Security, Backup) have significantly higher "stickiness."

### Technical Support Gaps
*   **Strategy**: Proactive Support Engagement.
*   **Tactics**:
    *   Offer a "Tech Concierge" session for elderly customers or high-data users.
    *   Priority Support status for 6 months if a customer reports a service issue.
*   **Rationale**: Poor technical support experience is a top driver for churning to competitors with better perceived service.

---

## 3. Product & Usage Optimization

### Low Tenure (< 12 Months)
*   **Strategy**: The "First-Year Reward" Program.
*   **Tactics**:
    *   Welcome bonus or credit at the 6-month mark.
    *   Free hardware upgrade (e.g., new router) after 12 months of consistent service.
*   **Rationale**: The first year is the most volatile period for a new customer.

### Fiber Optic Performance
*   **Strategy**: Service Stability Verification.
*   **Tactics**:
    *   Proactive network diagnostic call if Fiber Optic users exhibit high monthly charges but inconsistent usage patterns.
    *   Offer free mesh router setup for larger homes to maximize fiber speeds.
*   **Rationale**: High-paying Fiber customers expect premium reliability; any downtime leads to immediate frustration.

---

## 4. Competitive Positioning

### Competitor Pricing Matches
*   **Strategy**: "Loyalty Match" Program.
*   **Tactics**:
    *   Enable agents to match competitor "Introductory Offers" for existing customers.
    *   Focus on "Total Value" (Bundled services + Reliability) rather than just raw price.
